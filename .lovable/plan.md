
# Lyfex Education Platform - Homepage Development Plan

## Overview
Build a complete, interactive, and fully responsive homepage for the Lyfex education platform with a modern dark theme (navy blue background), orange/golden accent colors, and engaging educational content sections.

- **Lyfex logo** on the left with orange icon

## Design System
- **Primary background**: Dark navy blue (#0f172a)
- **Accent color**: Orange/Gold gradient (#f97316 to #fbbf24)
- **Card backgrounds**: Semi-transparent with blur effects

- **Border radius**: Rounded corners throughout
